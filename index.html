FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SEO, AEO, and what working with Find My Howl looks like.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)? +
AEO is the practice of optimizing your business so AI-powered tools — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Siri — recommend you when users ask relevant questions. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on a ranked list of links, AEO positions you as the definitive answer that AI serves directly. It's the most important new frontier in search, and most businesses aren't there yet.
What is the difference between SEO and AEO? +
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on ranking your website in traditional Google/Bing results pages. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) focuses on getting your business cited in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, Perplexity, and voice assistants. Both matter — SEO for sustained organic traffic, AEO for the rapidly growing AI search audience. We build strategies that cover both.
How long does SEO take to show results? +
Most small businesses see measurable SEO results within 60–90 days. Local SEO improvements — Google Business Profile optimization, local citations — can show results in 30 days. Full competitive keyword rankings typically take 4–6 months of consistent effort. We set honest expectations from day one and share progress every step of the way.
How much does small business SEO cost? +
Our Scout plan starts at $799/month for local SEO essentials. The Pack plan is $1,699/month for a full SEO and digital marketing strategy. Multi-location and enterprise businesses get custom pricing. All plans are month-to-month — no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees.
Do I need both SEO and AEO for my small business? +
Increasingly, yes. Traditional search still drives significant traffic so SEO is non-negotiable. But AI search tools are rapidly changing how people find local businesses — businesses that establish AEO authority now will have a meaningful competitive edge as AI search adoption grows. We include AEO foundations in every plan.
Does Find My Howl work with businesses outside of Denver? +
Yes. We're based in Denver, Colorado, but work with small businesses across the United States. Our services are fully remote and we have clients in multiple states. If you're a Denver business, we also offer in-person strategy sessions.
