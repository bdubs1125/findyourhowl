We were built for Main Street.

Our Story

Why we built this.

Find My Howl started with a simple observation: small businesses are getting left behind in the digital marketing revolution — not because they lack ambition, but because the agencies built to help them were designed for someone else.

Big agencies talk about brand strategy in glass-walled boardrooms. They build decks with a lot of slides and not a lot of answers. They measure success in impressions and awareness, not in phone calls and paying customers. And when a small business owner with a real budget constraint asks what they're actually getting for their money, the answer is usually a lot of jargon and not much clarity.

The baker on Main Street deserves the same digital edge as the brand with a million-dollar media budget.

We started Find My Howl because we believed small businesses deserve better — not a discount version of big-agency work, but something purpose-built: direct, honest, transparent, and relentlessly focused on results that matter to a real business owner.

The name comes from the way wolves communicate. A wolf doesn't wander alone hoping to stumble across its pack — it howls, sending a clear, powerful signal that travels across distance to reach exactly the right ears. That's what great digital marketing does. It helps your business send a signal so clear, so well-placed, and so well-targeted that the right customers find you — not just anyone, but the people who are already looking for exactly what you offer.

We're based in Denver, Colorado, and we work with small businesses across the United States. We specialize in Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) — the emerging practice of getting your business cited in AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews — and targeted paid digital marketing. We treat every client's budget like it's our own, because we know what it means to run a small business.

What We Actually Do

Three disciplines that work together to make sure the right customers find your business — wherever they're searching.

🔍

Search Engine Optimization

When someone searches for what you offer on Google, you should show up. SEO is the foundation — optimizing your site, your content, and your local presence so you rank where it counts. We do local SEO, technical audits, content strategy, Google Business Profile management, and everything else that moves the needle in traditional search.

🤖

Answer Engine Optimization

The way people find businesses is changing. ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Siri are increasingly the first place people go for recommendations — and they cite specific businesses in their answers. AEO is the practice of positioning your business to be the answer AI tools recommend. Most businesses haven't started yet. That's your advantage.

📣

Paid Digital Marketing

SEO and AEO build long-term visibility. Paid marketing gets you in front of customers right now. We run Google Ads, Meta campaigns, email marketing, and social content — targeted precisely at the customers most likely to become your best ones. Every dollar is tracked, and every campaign is built to convert, not just to run.
